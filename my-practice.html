My Practice

Hello!
I am a compassionate, supportive and collaborative therapist that offers both short term or long term therapy depending on what you need. I am here to provide you with a safe and nonjudgmental space to work through old pains and new life challenges with yourself or family members.

My areas of specialty include trauma, sexuality and sex, anxiety, depression, self esteem, stress, relationship issues and life adjustments / transitions. I have successfully helped clients become more confident in their bodies, emotionally stronger, and healthier overall in their lives.

I am trained in CBT / DBT, internal family systems, as well as mindfulness practices. I have found these modalities to be the most useful in assisting individuals, but I treat every individual as unique and am always open to collaborating and finding the best possible modality for you. My practice is rooted in inclusiveness and accessibility.

You can go to the contact section to learn more about booking a consultation, and the resources section contains hot-lines for people needing more immediate resources.

- Tara McMahon
